Frequently Asked Questions



What are websites? 

A website is a collection of web pages and related content that is published on at least one web server and given a common domain name. Websites are frequently devoted to a specific subject or objective, such as news, education, business, entertainment, or social networking.

Who creates websites? 

A web developer is someone who creates and updates a website's fundamental framework. A web developer must also be a seasoned designer that possesses a wide range of technical, graphic, and creative abilities.

Why does your business need it? 

In today's digital age, a small business needs a website to boost sales. Without a website, you lose most, if not all, of your potential customers because they won't be able to find out any information about you or your company's products or services.

Why choose Ezmasterdev? 

Ezmasterdev understands that in the age of digital marketing, it is of paramount importance that businesses regardless of scale, should have the opportunity to be able to present their brand online. Ezmasterdev create not with haste but with passion for a very friendly price.

When should your business have one? 

Without a website, customers could doubt your company's credibility. Having a website gives you the chance to make a fantastic first impression and reassure potential customers that you are a legitimate company. One of the most crucial things you can do is present your brand to potential buyers online. It is ideal to have one as soon as your business takes off.

How are websites made? 

A group of codes or programming languages are used to create websites. The most used languages for creating websites are: HTML, which enables browsers to display webpage elements including text, links, and media. Web page styles, including colors, fonts, and layouts, are defined using CSS.
